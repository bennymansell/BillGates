Here is a table of the 3 biggest snowboard companies and their attempts at addressing the climate change problems in their industry.


# Company Solutions Site
1 Burton "We use Super Sap® resin made with biobased materials, which reduces its carbon footprint by 33% compared to conventional, petroleum-based resins. Starting this winter, all Burton snowboards will be produced with Super Sap.®" https://www.burton.com/us/en/home
2 Mervin Manufacturing Mervin uses better manufacturing practices that both protect the health of the Kraftsmen, but also that of the planet, leaving zero hazardous waste behind in production. https://www.mervinmade.com/#map
3 Never Summer Boards Never Summer tries to reduce waste in each phase of the manufacturing process. The only waste from wood used for the core of the board is sawdust, as all boards are cut to length and all other trimmings are used in youth snowboards. https://www.neversummer.com/
